Fill

Fill

Uses only part of the image that fills the given dimensions. Only part of the original image might be visible if the required proportions are different than the original ones.

Mixes In

AdjustMixin, FilterMixin, AlignmentMixin, WidthHeightQualityMixin, ResizeFillMixin, OperationMixin
